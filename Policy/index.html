Privacy Policy

OrderPilot Pro - WooCommerce Order Notification Manager

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how OrderPilot Pro ("the App", "we", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application. OrderPilot Pro is a companion app for WooCommerce store owners and staff to receive, view, and manage order notifications from their own WordPress/WooCommerce store.

By installing and using OrderPilot Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Store Connection Information

When you connect the App to your WooCommerce store, you provide a Store Code (a 6-digit code generated by the OrderPilot Pro WordPress plugin) and a Staff Name of your choosing. This information is used solely to authenticate your device with your store and identify which staff member is using the App.

1.2 Device Identifier

The App generates a random, anonymous Device ID on first launch. This identifier is stored locally on your device and used to link your device to your store account for authentication and to deliver push notifications to the correct device. It does not contain any personally identifiable information and cannot be used to identify you outside of the App.

1.3 Push Notification Token

To deliver real-time order alerts, the App registers your device with our push-notification provider (OneSignal, which uses Firebase Cloud Messaging on Android). This generates a unique push token associated with your device, which is used exclusively to send you order notifications.

1.4 Order & Store Data

Once connected, the App retrieves order information from your WooCommerce store through our secure backend API, including order numbers, customer names, phone numbers, delivery addresses, product details, prices, order status, and notes. This data belongs to your store and is displayed within the App so you can manage orders. This data is transmitted securely (HTTPS) and is not shared with any third party other than as required to operate the App (see Section 3).

1.5 Crash & Diagnostic Logs

If the App encounters an unexpected error, a diagnostic log (technical error details and stack trace) is saved locally on your device only. This log is never transmitted automatically. You may choose to view, copy, and manually share this log with the developer for troubleshooting purposes, or clear it at any time from the Developer screen.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate your device and connect it to your WooCommerce store
  • To deliver real-time push notifications for new and updated orders
  • To display, search, filter, and let you manage your store's orders
  • To detect and prevent unauthorized access (e.g., proxy/VPN checks before sensitive requests)
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues (using locally stored crash logs that you choose to share)

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or analytics tracking, and the App contains no third-party advertising SDKs.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Connection credentials and order data are stored locally on your device using secure app storage, and are not included in cloud or device backups (the App disables Android's automatic backup for app data).
  • All network communication between the App and our backend uses encrypted HTTPS connections only. Unencrypted (HTTP) traffic is blocked.
  • The App actively detects active VPN or proxy connections and blocks sensitive requests when one is detected, as an additional security measure to protect your store's data.
  • Access to your store's data requires the Store Code generated from your own WordPress admin panel, which only you and your staff control.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Locally stored data (connection details, cached orders, crash logs) remains on your device until you disconnect your store, clear the App's data, or uninstall the App. Disconnecting your store from the Settings screen removes your locally stored authentication tokens and connection details.

Order data fetched from your WooCommerce store remains under your control and is managed through your own WordPress admin panel. To request deletion of any data associated with your store on our backend, please contact us using the details in Section 9.

7. Children's Privacy

OrderPilot Pro is intended for business use by store owners and staff and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
